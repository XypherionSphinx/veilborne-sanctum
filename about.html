Crown of Silence

“Who I really am, beneath it all.”

I walk between ruins and riddles, weaving silence into story. This world I’ve built — Veilborne Sanctum — is made of masks, but this page is not one of them.

(Plain English: This is the ‘About Me’ page.)

I go by many names online — the Crimson Veilborne, Hollow Thorn, and others — but behind them is one voice: a writer, worldbuilder, and artist exploring memory, identity, and myth through digital spaces.

I build this site as both sanctuary and stage: a place where unfinished things still matter, where creation and ruin walk hand-in-hand. If you're here, you've wandered into something deeply personal. You're welcome to stay.

What this site is: A digital shrine to everything I’ve made, forgotten, or never quite finished. It’s also a portfolio, a storyworld, and a mask I speak through.

Tech stack? Mostly HTML, CSS, and custom design. I may add JS, sound, or game systems later.

⸺ I am not what I make. But it remembers me. ⸺
